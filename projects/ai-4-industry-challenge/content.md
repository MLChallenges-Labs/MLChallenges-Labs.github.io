[AI4Industry Challenge](https://www.dassault-aviation.com/en/group/press/press-kits/dassault-aviation-partners-the-paris-region-challenge-ai-for-industry-2020/) , organized in collaboration with Dassault-Aviation and Ile-de-France (Paris Region), is an ambitious time series regression contest from aeronautical data for predictive maintenance, where Gradient Boosted Trees methods have excelled.

The winning team, Aquila Data Enabler, won a 500,000 € research grant and started a collaboration with Dassault-Aviation in order to pursue the research and deploy their solution on production systems.

For more details, you can read the scientific paper, published at ICMLA conference: [[Link](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9680054)]
