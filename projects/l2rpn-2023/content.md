The Learning to Run a Power Network (L2RPN) 2023 challenge addresses climate change by promoting the energy transition to renewable sources like solar and wind. Organized with Réseau de Transport de l’Électricité (RTE) and Ile-de-France (Paris Region), the participants competed over a 500,000 € research grant, developping AI agents able to operate a simulated power grid, in a reinforcement-learning-like protocol.

Competition website: [[Link](https://www.codabench.org/competitions/1559/)]

For more detail, you can read the analysis paper, published at “Energy and AI” journal: [[Link](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2666546825000965)]
